Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a surveillance tool?

No. Writeful only tracks what happens inside the writing editor. No webcam, no screen recording, no keystroke logging outside the app. Students see everything that's tracked.

Can students use AI tools?

That's up to the teacher. Writeful doesn't block AI — it records the writing process so teachers can see how the essay was produced. Each assignment can have its own AI policy.

What about students who type slowly or have disabilities?

Writeful measures process, not speed. A slow typist who revises thoughtfully shows a rich, human process. The system adapts to individual patterns, not arbitrary benchmarks.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Writeful is a PWA that works offline. Writing and tracking continue without a connection. Everything syncs when the student reconnects. No work is ever lost.

Does Writeful label essays as "AI-generated"?

Never. Writeful shows the process data. The teacher makes the call. No automated accusations, no false positives, no algorithmic judgements.

What data is stored?

Document snapshots, typing timing patterns (not individual keystrokes), paste events, session timestamps, and revision history. All data is encrypted and FERPA/COPPA compliant.
